Who Are We?

Milty's Paintball is the premier location for Paintball in Maryville, Missouri! Located just off of Highway 71, Milty's is an expansive multiple-field facility. As an exhilarating activty , paintball involves physical and tactical prowess unlike any sport! Come and show your Skill At Milty's Paintball.

Our state of the art fields provide multiple ways to play. The heartpounding action and tactics of speedball provide short but intense matches. While the slower and more methodical woodsball will bring out the hunter in you. Milty's paintball is well stocked with rental gear to provide a thrilling afternoon. Of course you can bring your own gear as well!
